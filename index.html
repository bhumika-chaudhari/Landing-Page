Get to Know Us

Raw Cocoa Fruit

The journey starts with the raw cocoa fruit, harvested by hand from tropical cacao trees. Inside each pod, cocoa beans are nestled in a sweet, tangy pulp. These beans are the beginning of our chocolate story.

Ripe Cocoa Fruit

As the cocoa fruit ripens, it changes color and is ready for harvesting. The ripe fruit is opened to reveal the valuable cocoa beans inside. These beans are crucial for making chocolate.

Cocoa Powder

After harvesting, the cocoa beans are fermented, dried, roasted, and processed into cocoa powder. This fine, rich powder is essential for creating various chocolate treats. It's the base of all our chocolate products.

Chocolate

Finally, the cocoa powder is blended with other ingredients to create smooth, creamy Handmade chocolate. We transform raw cocoa into delicious chocolate bars and treats. Each piece is made with love and care
